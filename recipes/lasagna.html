Lasagna

This enchilada lasagna is perfect for dinner or potlucks.

This dish takes about 20 minutes to prepare and cooks for 1 hour and 15 minutes with a serving of 8.

Per Serving: 428 calories; protein 31g; carbohydrates 22.1g; fat 24.1g; cholesterol 120mg; sodium 929.9mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 ¼ pounds ground turkey
  • 1 (28 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with lime juice and cilantro
  • 1 (16 ounce) package small-curd cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 5 (6 inch) corn tortillas, halved
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • cooking spray
  • 1 green onion, diced
